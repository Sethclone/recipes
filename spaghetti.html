Description:

A nice mamma mia plate of a spaghetti good for the whole family

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Take your spaghetti and boil it
  2. Put your pasta sauce in a pot
  3. Once the spaghetti is boiled place it in the pot containing the sauce
  4. Plate it and top with parmesan and basil
